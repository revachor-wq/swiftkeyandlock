Our Story

Stuart FL's
Local Locksmith.

Swift Key & Lock was founded on a simple belief: every person deserves a fast, honest, and professional locksmith. No bait-and-switch pricing. No call centers. No strangers who don't know your neighborhood.

We are a locally operated mobile locksmith serving Stuart FL and the entire Treasure Coast. When you call, you reach a real technician — not a dispatcher in another state. We know every street, every neighborhood, every zip code from Palm City to Jupiter.

Over 5,000 jobs and 15+ years later, our promise remains the same: or less, zero damage, upfront pricing — every single time.
