FAQ — Treasure Coast

Questions About
Treasure Coast Locksmith.

Everything you need to know before calling.

How fast can a locksmith get to me on the Treasure Coast? +
Swift Key & Lock serves the entire Treasure Coast — Port St. Lucie, Stuart, Jensen Beach, Palm City, Hobe Sound, and Fort Pierce. We are typically on-site in 20–30 minutes.
How much does a locksmith cost? +
Pricing depends on the service and your specific situation. We always provide a firm, upfront quote before any work begins — no surprises, no hidden fees. Call us for a free quote.
Do you serve the entire Treasure Coast 24 hours a day? +
Yes. Swift Key & Lock serves the entire Treasure Coast 24/7/365 — including Port St. Lucie, Stuart, Jensen Beach, Palm City, Hobe Sound, and Fort Pierce — with the same flat rates day and night. No after-hours fees, no weekend surcharges, no holiday markups.
Are you licensed to work as a locksmith on the Treasure Coast? +
Yes. Every Swift Key & Lock technician is fully licensed by the State of Florida, bonded, covered by $1M general liability insurance, and has passed a criminal background check. We are BBB A+ accredited and available upon request for verification.
Can you make a car key without the original? +
Yes. We can cut and program replacement transponder keys, smart keys, and key fobs for most vehicle makes and models on-site anywhere on the Treasure Coast — no towing required. We carry equipment for Toyota, Honda, Ford, GM, BMW, Mercedes, and most others.
What areas of the Treasure Coast do you serve? +
We serve all of the Treasure Coast including Port St. Lucie, Stuart, Jensen Beach, Palm City, Hobe Sound, and Fort Pierce — plus surrounding neighborhoods throughout St. Lucie and Martin counties.
